    🎥 Streamer Program

    Become a ValkariaRO Creator

    Ready to turn your gameplay into influence?

    ValkariaRO is opening the doors to all streamers who want to grow with us.
    Whether you're just starting out or already building an audience, this is your chance to stream, earn rewards, and become part of something bigger.

    🎥 Stream your adventure
    💎 Earn rewards based on hours
    🚀 Unlock official Streamer Code opportunities

    No follower requirement. No platform restriction.
    Just stream, have fun, and help us grow the ValkariaRO world.

    🚀 Program Overview

    We are inviting content creators to help promote ValkariaRO.

    You may stream on:

    • 🎥 Facebook Gaming
    • 🟣 Twitch
    • ▶️ YouTube
    • 🎵 TikTok
    • Any other streaming platform

    Info

    There is no minimum follower requirement to join.

    📋 Participation Requirements

    Mandatory Streaming Format

    All participants must follow the official stream format.

    ✔ On-Screen Requirements

    • ValkariaRO Official Logo must be visible at all times. You may grab the logo here. Click Here

    • The following text must be clearly displayed:

      www.valkariaro.com

    • Stream title must include:

      Playing ValkariaRO | Pre-Renewal 99/70

    • Below is the sample for your stream screen. Position for both logo and text is up to your likings.

    • No toxic, racist, religious, or offensive commentary

    Failure to follow the format may result in reward rejection.

    ⏱️ Streaming Reward Structure

    Note

    Rewards are granted per approved streaming session.

    Stream Duration Tier Reward
    2+ Hours 🥉 Bronze Streamer 20 CP
    3+ Hours 🥈 Silver Streamer 40 CP
    5+ Hours 🥇 Gold Streamer 80 CP
    6+ Hours 💎 Diamond Streamer 120 CP

    Final reward details will be announced by the Valkaria Team. Rewards also can be changed anytime by the Team.

    👥 Active Viewer Bonus

    Additional rewards are granted based on your Active Viewers.

    Active viewers are viewers who actively watch and interact with the stream.

    Active Viewers Bonus Reward
    5+ Active Viewers +10 CP
    10+ Active Viewers +20 CP
    20+ Active Viewers +40 CP

    💬 Engagement Bonus

    Additional rewards are granted based on stream engagement and community interaction.

    Comments Bonus Reward
    10+ Comments +10 CP
    25+ Comments +20 CP

    Tip

    Real interaction and communication with viewers are encouraged. If we found any suspicious behaviour in comment section, no rewards will be given

    🎟️ Reward Claim Process

    Step Action Required Information
    🟢 Step 1 Start Your Stream Open a Discord ticket immediately after starting your stream.
    📌 Submit the following: • Stream Link
    • Streaming Platform
    • In-Game Character Name
    🔴 Step 2 End Your Stream Reply to the same Discord ticket after your stream ends.
    📊 Submit your stream analytics: • Stream Link
    • Screenshot of Stream Analytics
    • Stream Duration
    • Active Viewer Statistics
    • Comment / Engagement Statistics
    🖼️ Example Screenshot
    🟡 Step 3 Review & Verification Valkaria Team will review your submission and verify:

    • Stream Duration
    • Viewer Activity
    • Engagement Statistics
    • Stream Legitimacy
    🏆 Step 4 Reward Distribution Once approved, rewards will be distributed to your in-game account.

    Tip

    To speed up verification, please ensure all screenshots are clear and analytics are fully visible.

    Note

    Submitting incomplete information may delay reward processing. We will close the ticket every Sunday Midnight for rewards distributions.

    💰 Streamer Code Program (To be announced soon)

    Selected streamers may qualify for an Official Streamer Code.

    Benefits

    • 5% commission from purchases using your code
    • Special Discord role
    • Featured recognition
    • Collaboration opportunities

    📊 Streamer Code Eligibility

    To qualify:

    • Consistent streaming activity
    • Positive viewer engagement
    • Professional stream quality
    • No rule violations
    • Genuine audience (no artificial boosting)

    Success

    Streamer Code approval is reviewed manually by the Valkaria Team.

    🚫 Disqualification Policy

    You may be removed from the program if:

    • Streaming format is not followed
    • Fake viewers or artificial boosting is detected
    • Toxic behavior is observed
    • Misleading server promotion occurs

    🎯 Program Objective

    This program is designed to:

    • Increase ValkariaRO visibility
    • Support content creators
    • Grow the community
    • Create a win-win partnership model
