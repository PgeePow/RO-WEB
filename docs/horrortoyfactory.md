# üß≠ How to Start

4. Enter through the **Factory Dimension Teleporter**.

---

# üéÑ First Phase
!!! tip "Transformation Mechanics"
    - Talking to NPCs without being a **Red Cookie** spawns **Nightmare Cruisers**.
    - Use Myst Case NPCs around the map to refresh your transformation timer.

### Objectives
- Transform into **Red Cookie**.
- Kill **Vicious Cookies** and **Evil Dwelling Boxes**.
- After enough kills, all mobs die automatically.
- Speak to **Green Cookie** in the northwest (must be Red Cookie).
- Transform into **Myst Case**.
- Enter the **right portal**.

---

# üç™ Second Phase
- Talk to Myst Case NPC ‚Üí transform into **Red Cookie**.
- Speak to all **10 Green Cookie workers**.
- You cannot move or use skills/consumables during their dialogue.
- Monsters can still attack.
- After all 10 are spoken to, mobs die automatically.
- Return through the original portal ‚Üí take **north portal**.

---

# üéÖ Third Phase
- Enter the center to trigger dialogue between **Santa** and **Antonio**.
- Enter the **left portal**.
- Kill **Antonio**.
- Antonio may spawn in the previous room‚Äîcheck both.
- After killing him, mobs disappear.
- Return to center ‚Üí take **right portal**.

---

# üíÄ Boss Phase ‚Äì Celine Kimi
!!! danger "Boss Overview"
    **Celine Kimi** (Large ‚Ä¢ Ghost 1 ‚Ä¢ Undead)

### Mechanics
- **Max Pain (10s)** ‚Äì Reflects all attacks screen-wide.  
  Do **NOT** attack during this.
- Indicators: Long cast + `/hp` emoticon.

### Other Abilities
- Pulse Strike
- Power Up (15k+ hits possible)
- Critical Wounds
- Wide Soul Drain (`/mp`)
- Wide Stone Curse
- Wide Sleep

### Phantom Mechanics
- Must keep **Celine Kimi and Phantom together**.
- If separated ‚Üí Phantom heals her massively.
- HP difference >1% ‚Üí Phantom heals her **6% max HP**.
- Dragging boss too far resets the fight.

!!! tip "Positioning"
    Fight her near the **upper wall** to reduce knockback and prevent resets.
