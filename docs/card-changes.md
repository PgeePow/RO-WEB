# Card Changes

To improve build diversity and create more interesting gearing options, several cards have been adjusted from their official behavior. These changes are designed to make underused cards more viable while maintaining overall balance for both PvE and PvP environments.

Below is the full list of customized card adjustments currently available on the server.

| Card ID | Card Name | Previous Behaviour | Current Behaviour |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4521 | Sedora Card | Official Behavior | Crit Damage +15%<br>Critical +3<br>Every physical attack restores 10 HP |
| 4522 | Sropho Card | Official Behavior | Vit +1<br>MaxHP +5%<br>Chance to cast Frost Nova when being hit<br>Card slot changed from Weapon ➜ Shoes |
| 4524 | King Dramoh Card | Official Behavior | STR +1<br>(Swordman & Swordman Upper Classes)<br>For every 2 refine on compounded item, STR +1 |
| 4525 | Kraken Card | Official Behavior | Flee +10 ➜ +20 |
| 4131 | Moonlight Flower Card | Official Behavior | Agi +3<br>Flee +10<br>Movement Speed increased by 40%<br>(Does not stack with other Movement Speed effects) |
| 4428 | Bow Guardian Card | Official Behavior | If compounded on a Bow:<br>Arrow Shower Damage +40%<br>ATK +5<br>HIT +5<br>Double Strafe Damage +7% if user has 99 Base DEX |
| 4144 | Osiris Card | Official Behavior | Restores 100% HP and SP upon resurrection<br>Neutral Resistance +10%<br>Undead Resistance +10% |
| 4148 | Pharaoh Card | Official Behavior | Reduces SP cost of skills by 30%<br>MaxHP +1000<br>MDEF +25 |
