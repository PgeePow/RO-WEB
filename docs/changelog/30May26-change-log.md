## 🎯 Monster Drop System Adjustment

The monster ownership system has been updated.

### Previous Behavior

A player could deal partial damage to a monster and potentially retain ownership of the drops.

### New Behavior

The player who delivers the **killing blow** will receive the item drops.

!!! tip "Goodbye Half-HP Tagging"

    No more hitting a monster to half HP and running away. ❤️
