Warper System

Summary

The Warper System in our server is designed to encourage exploration and adventure. Unlike traditional warpers that grant access to all locations instantly, players must unlock each warp point by visiting the corresponding town or dungeon. This system ensures a more immersive experience while maintaining balanced gameplay.

How It Works

  1. Discovering a Warp Point
  2. To unlock a location, players must physically visit the warp point in a town or dungeon.

  3. Once interacted with, the location becomes available in the warper menu.

  4. Using the Warper NPC

  5. After unlocking a warp point, visit the Warper NPC in major cities.

  6. Select from your list of unlocked locations to teleport instantly. 5000z Teleport fee will be charge for each teleport.

  7. Warp Categories

  8. Warps are categorized into Towns, and Dungeons.
  9. Some high-level dungeons require additional requirements or quest completions.
